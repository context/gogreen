Founders

MARTIN GUTIERREZ

Martin has held senior management positions with companies in the communications industry for over 17 years. He has a wealth of experience in sales, marketing, business development, and mergers & acquisitions in wireless infrastructure, application development, and digital media sectors. Martin founded First Catalyst, LLC, an independent management consulting firm that provides technical and operational assistance to corporations and investment firms in the mergers and acquisitions (M&A) process. Prior to First Catalyst, Martin was Vice President of Business Development with Aether Systems. Prior to Aether, Martin worked with Ericsson as Senior Director of Worldwide Business Development. At Ericsson, Martin secured and developed strategic alliance relationships with leading systems integrators, software companies and platform vendors. His efforts were instrumental in securing significant infrastructure contracts with carriers in Europe, the US, and Latin America. Aside from his responsibilities at First Catalyst, LLC, and the Go Green Foundation, Martin enjoys many outdoor activities with his wife and two children; Margaret, Catalina and Trevor, including mountain biking, body boarding, camping, running, and tennis.

MARGARET TAKEI-GUTIERREZ

Margaret has held various executive level positions in operations management in advertising, real estate and non-profit organizations. Margaret launched her career in San Francisco with J Walter Thompson, which is one of the most famous names in advertising history. It was arguably the first worldwide advertising agency, although it now prefers the term global brand communications. Margaret moved on to TRI Realtors where she reported to the CEO and Chairman. Margaret is currently working with Susie Tompkins Buell and Mark Buell, philanthropists and political activists in San Francisco. Margaret is a working mom. Her children, Trevor & Catalina, inspired the creation of the Go Green Foundation. Aside from her responsibilities with Susie Tompkins Buell and Mark Buell and the Go Green Foundation, Margaret also enjoys the outdoors, camping, biking, hiking, running, and tennis.

CATALINA GUTIERREZ

Catalina has a genuine love of nature and enjoys competing in a variety of sports, outdoor activities, and performance arts. It was Catalina’s inspiration that nurtured the concept and ultimate naming of the Go Green Foundation. Catalina is a diligent, conscientious, impassioned, and well-respected member of her community. She is a founder of the Go Green Foundation Teen Advisor Group and works with her peers to create, implement, and monitor sustainable living practice initiatives at her school. As MVP of her junior varsity volleyball team, she led her team to the City’s Independent School League championship; her varsity basketball team was also undefeated and was runner up in the city championship. Catalina also studied ballet and performed at the San Francisco Ballet under the tutelage and artistic direction of Helgi Tomasson. Catalina enjoys classical, latin jazz, pop rock, and jazz She studies keyboards at the Blue Bear School of Music. Catalina is currently attending Katherine Delmar Burke School in San Francisco.

TREVOR GUTIERREZ

Trevor enjoys athletics and has a deep appreciation of the outdoors. He has a very active and vivid imagination, and inspired the development of the Go Green Foundation School of Sustainability and Social Entrepreneurship Virtual World. Trevor is an engaging, empathetic, conscientious, and well-respected member of his school community. He is a founder of the Go Green Foundation Teen Advisory Group and works diligently with his peers to develop, implement, and monitor sustainable living practice initiatives at his current school. Trevor has enjoyed early success in his academic and athletic development. In his first Little League Baseball season, Trevor led his team as the starting pitcher to the finals and was nominated by his peers and coaches to the All-Star team. Trevor is the consummate team player. Last season his basketball team at Cathedral School for Boys was undefeated and runner up in the San Francisco Independent School League championship. Trevor enjoys jazz, rock, latin, and classical music and studies guitar at the Blue Bear School of Music. Trevor is currently attending Cathedral School for Boys in San Francisco.
