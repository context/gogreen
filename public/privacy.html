Go Green Foundation Privacy Policy

Go Green Foundation collects names and email addresses in order to communicate with our users about their pledges to improve the environment. This information will not be sold for any purpose, but may be shared with partner organizations such as the Environmental Defense Fund and the Climate Counts Initiative. At no point will the contact information provided to our site be made public.

It is our policy that children under age 13 using our site should supply the email address of a parent, in order to ensure parental and consent to all communications. Further questions should be directed to
